Store Rules

Card Trade-Ins

  • Only the Banker may process card trade-ins to ensure there are no discrepancies.
  • When trading in cards, send your list to the designated note page.
  • Players should update their list while playing to ensure faster processing.

Credit System

  • Players may trade in any cards from their binder in exchange for credits.
  • The maximum credit balance is 1000 credits.

Purchasing Items

  • At the end of player rewards being handed out after phases complete,
  • each player will get the chance to spend as many credits as they want to.
