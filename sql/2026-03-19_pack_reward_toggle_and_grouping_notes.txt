2026-03-19 Pack reward toggle and grouping

- Replaced the old `RWD` pack-number special case with a dedicated `is_reward_pack` flag.
- Pack numbering is now:
  - `001-999` for Normal Packs
  - `001-999` for Reward Packs
- Removed the internal Set organizer from the live pack admin flow.
- Pack picker grouping is now driven by two internal categories:
  - `Normal Packs`
  - `Reward Packs`
- Added `public.containers.is_reward_pack boolean not null default false`.
- Added a unique full-pack product scope on `(is_reward_pack, pack_number_code)` so number collisions are prevented within each category.
- Any legacy `RWD`-numbered pack products are migrated into the reward category and assigned sequential `001+` reward numbers.
