Yu-Gi-Oh Hub

This hub is the main home for our Gentlemen's Gaming Yu-Gi-Oh formats, community play, and future tools. Right now, our two core modes are Progression Series and Deck Game.

Progression Series is based on the popular Yu-Gi-Oh Progression format, where players open sealed product in release order, expand their available card pool over time, and play best-of-three matches with decks built only from what they have opened. Traditional Progression rules commonly use 24 packs from a main set per round, expand the card pool set by set, and play a best-of-three match after deck construction. Our version follows that spirit, but uses a custom Gentlemen's Gaming ruleset, custom phases, and our own structure rather than copying standard Progression 1:1.

Deck Game is our curated ladder-style format. Think of it like Gun Game from Call of Duty, but with Yu-Gi-Oh decks instead of weapons. Players compete in best 2 out of 3 matches using a prepared list of curated decks, and after beating one deck, they move on to the next. The goal is to keep advancing through the lineup by winning matches and climbing through the deck order.
